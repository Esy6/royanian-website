About Us

 

Our Company was founded in 2012 in Vienna, which has expanded its activities in import and export of goods and machines of all kinds. We are specialized in activities trading of products, devices, equipment, furniture, accessories and consumables for the sectors of dental, medical, cosmetic, beauty as well as agriculture and renewable energy segment.
We provide intelligent, reasonably priced and absolutely reliable professional products in order to support more suitable and easier treatments in dental labs, medical clinics and hospitals as well as in the agriculture business unit.
